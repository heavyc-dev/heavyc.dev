Overview

A real terminal, built for the phone.

Existing mobile SSH apps treat the phone like a tiny, awkward desktop. node.dev is designed from the ground up for the phone — clean, tactile, and key-safe — with an AI agent that lets you describe what you want instead of thumb-typing commands.

$_

Full terminal

A genuine xterm-compatible shell with proper key handling and scrollback. Your real shell, your real tools — not a stripped-down toy.

Files

Browse, view, and edit files on your machine over SFTP, straight from the phone.

Git at a glance

See the current branch, working-tree status, and recent commits without typing a command.

Forward a port from your machine to your phone to preview a local dev server or test an API on the go.

Codie

An AI coding agent that runs on your machine.

Codie is a chat-based AI coding agent that lives on your phone but runs on your machine. Describe what you want in natural language; Codie carries it out on your actual codebase — reading files, running commands, making edits, working with git — and reports back in a clean conversational thread.

Real work, real machine

Codie operates on your live project, not a sandbox or a copy. The results are real changes you can review and ship.

You stay in control

Codie asks for approval before sensitive actions, and you choose its access per project — read-only, edit and git, or full autonomy.

Transparent

Each turn shows what it did, how long it took, and what it cost — no surprises.

Personable

Codie has an animated character and a conversational voice. On supported iPhones, Apple Intelligence runs entirely on-device — purely for conversation, never to make decisions about your code.

How it works

From zero to coding in three steps.

The phone is the control surface. Your PC does the heavy lifting. node.dev keeps the two in sync over an encrypted private link.

Install a private network

node.dev connects to your PC over Tailscale, a zero-config encrypted mesh network. Your devices talk directly and privately — nothing exposed to the public internet.

One-time PC setup

A single guided script installs an SSH server on your Windows PC, locks it to your private network, and accepts a key from your phone automatically. No passwords, no manual file editing.

Connect and work

From then on, your phone has a secure, key-only connection to your machine. Open a terminal, browse files, forward a port — or hand the work to Codie.

Security & privacy

A foundation, not a bolt-on.

Private by default

All traffic runs over your own encrypted Tailscale network. Your machine is never exposed to the public internet.

Keys never leave the device

Modern Ed25519 SSH keys are stored in the iPhone's hardware-backed Keychain, locked behind Face ID / Touch ID. The private key is never uploaded or synced.

No passwords

The PC is configured for key-only authentication. Only your phone can connect.

No cloud, no telemetry

node.dev has no backend. Sessions, history, and diagnostics stay on your device. Nothing is collected or phoned home.
