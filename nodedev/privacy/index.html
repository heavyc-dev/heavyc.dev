Privacy Policy

Your data stays yours.

Last updated: May 28, 2026

In short: node.dev has no backend and no servers. It collects no analytics or telemetry. Your SSH keys, connection details, session history, and the work you do all stay on your own devices and your own machine. Nothing is sent to Heavy C Development Studio.

Who this covers

This policy describes how the node.dev iPhone app, published by Heavy C Development Studio, LLC ("we", "us"), handles your information. It applies to the app and this website.

What we collect

Nothing. node.dev has no account system, no backend service, and no analytics SDKs. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. We have no servers that could receive your data.

Data that stays on your device

node.dev stores the following locally on your iPhone, never transmitted to us:

  • SSH keys. Your private key is generated on-device and stored in the iPhone's hardware-backed Keychain, locked behind Face ID / Touch ID. It is never uploaded, synced, or exported by the app.
  • Host and connection details. The hostnames, IPs, usernames, and ports you save stay in local app storage.
  • Session history and terminal scrollback. Kept on the device for your convenience and never sent off it.
  • App settings and project organization. Stored locally.

How your connection works

node.dev connects to your own computer directly over Tailscale, an encrypted private mesh network. Your traffic goes from your phone to your PC — it does not pass through us. Your machine is never exposed to the public internet.

Tailscale is a third-party service with its own privacy policy. node.dev does not control Tailscale and does not receive any data from it.

Codie (AI agent)

Codie runs on your machine and operates on your local codebase. Commands and file changes happen on your own computer. On supported iPhones, Apple Intelligence may run on-device to make Codie's conversational voice sound natural — this processing stays on your device and is used only for conversation, never to make decisions about your code or to send your code anywhere.

Crash logs and diagnostics

Any crash logs or diagnostics are stored locally and are shared only if you explicitly choose to send them. We do not collect them automatically.

Children's privacy

node.dev is a developer tool not directed at children, and because we collect no data, we do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the "last updated" date above.
