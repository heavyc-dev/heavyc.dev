What to test

  • App launch, update, and install behavior.
  • ROM loading with files you legally provide.
  • Save state behavior, in-game saves, and resume flow.
  • Tracker flow for team, routes, deaths, badges, and level caps.
  • Anything that feels slow, confusing, or brittle.
bug_report

How to report issues

Email dev@heavyc.dev with your platform, build version, what you expected, what happened, and the shortest steps that reproduce the issue.

Do not send ROMs, BIOS files, or copyrighted game files in bug reports.

LockeBox alpha builds are unfinished software. Back up your saves before testing, expect bugs, and only use game files and BIOS files that you are legally allowed to use.
