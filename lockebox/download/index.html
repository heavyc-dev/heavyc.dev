Getting started

  • Install LockeBox for your platform.
  • Provide a GBA ROM you legally own.
  • Start a run and let the tracker sync from your save.
  • Log encounters, deaths, badges, and level caps as you play.
bug_report

Report an issue

Email dev@heavyc.dev with your platform, version, what you expected, what happened, and the shortest steps that reproduce the issue.

Do not send ROMs, BIOS files, or copyrighted game files in reports.

LockeBox does not include ROMs or BIOS files. Only use game files and BIOS files that you are legally allowed to use.
