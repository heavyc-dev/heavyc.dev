Privacy Policy

Your save stays with you.

Last updated: July 9, 2026

In short: Hearthbound: Legacy is a single-player game with no account and no sign-in. Your progress is saved on your own device. The game is free and shows optional rewarded ads through Google AdMob; an optional one-time purchase removes them. We don't build user profiles or sell your data.

Who this covers

This policy explains how the Hearthbound: Legacy mobile game and this website, published by Heavy C Development Studio, LLC ("we", "us"), handle information. By playing the game or using this site, you agree to this policy.

Data stored on your device

The game keeps your progress locally on your device. There is no account, no login, and no cloud profile. Locally-stored data includes:

  • Game progress — your bloodline, characters, levels, skill trees, unlocked maps and pets, crafted items, and currencies.
  • Settings — audio, camera, quality, and control preferences.

This data is not transmitted to us. Deleting the app removes it. Platform features you control (such as your device's own backup or restore) may include the app's local files, per your device settings.

Advertising

The game is free to play and supported by rewarded ads served through Google AdMob. To deliver and cap ads and to measure them, Google and its ad partners may collect and process information such as a device advertising identifier, coarse device and app information, and general (non-precise) location. We do not receive this data as individual profiles.

  • On first launch, where required, you'll see a consent prompt (Google's UMP) and, on iOS, Apple's App Tracking Transparency request. Your choices there govern how ads use your data.
  • Google's handling of ad data is described in the Google Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from sites/apps that use its services.
  • Buying the optional ad-free purchase (below) removes ads entirely.

Purchases

The game offers optional in-app purchases, including a one-time purchase that removes ads and grants an equivalent free revive. Payments are handled entirely by the Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see or store your payment card or billing details — the store processes the transaction and tells the app only whether you own the item.

Analytics & crash diagnostics

We may use aggregate, non-identifying analytics and crash diagnostics — provided by the app stores and standard tooling — to fix bugs and balance the game (for example, counts of runs completed or crashes on a build). This information is not used to identify you personally, and we do not combine it into individual profiles.

What we don't do

  • No account, email, name, or phone number is required or collected by us.
  • We don't sell your personal information.
  • We don't knowingly collect precise location.
  • Beyond the advertising described above (governed by your consent choices), we don't track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Children's privacy

Hearthbound: Legacy is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through our advertising or store integrations, contact us and we will address it.

Your choices

  • Ads: change or reset your ad-consent choice from the device's privacy settings, reset or limit your advertising identifier, or buy the ad-free purchase to remove ads.
  • All local data: delete the app to erase your save and settings from the device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and revise the "last updated" date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect in a new release.
