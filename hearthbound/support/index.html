Frequently asked

How is my progress saved?

Automatically, on your device. There's no account or sign-in — your bloodline lives in the app's local save. It's fully playable offline.

I got a new phone / lost my progress.

Because saves are stored locally, progress is tied to the device unless it was included in your platform's own device backup. Cloud sync between devices isn't available yet.

How do I remove ads?

Buy the optional one-time ad-free purchase in the game's shop. It removes rewarded-ad prompts and grants an equivalent free revive.

I bought the ad-free purchase but don't have it.

Use Restore Purchases in the game, and make sure you're signed into the same App Store / Google Play account used for the purchase. If it still doesn't appear, email us with the store receipt.

Found a bug, or have feedback?

Please tell us — it genuinely helps. Email dev@heavyc.dev with what happened, what you expected, and your device + game version.
