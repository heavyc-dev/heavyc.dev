Mobile survivors-like · lineage & permadeath

Your hero will fall.

Hearthbound: Legacy is a one-thumb survivors-like built around a hearth that outlives every run. Carve through swarms, fall to the dark, then pass the flame to an heir who inherits your legacy. Every death makes the next life stronger.

✦Lineage & permadeath

Death is the loop, not the end. A ritual passes your account-wide legacy — insight, might, unlocks — to the next heir. Fallen ancestors even return to fight beside you.

✦Classes & skill trees

Six classes, each with three branches and build-defining keystones and subclass capstones. Chase the fantasy that fits your run — bloodied brawler, kiting hunter, mobile assassin.

✦Living pets

Raise animal companions that fight, tank, loot, and buff — bonding, aging, and breeding across generations of your bloodline.

✦Maps & bosses

Push through Verdant Plains, Sunscorch Badlands, and Frostreach Tundra — each with its own swarm, its own beasts, and a boss that gates the way forward.

✦Forge & Reliquary

Smelt materials into tiered weapons and heirlooms, socket runes, and tap the Reliquary for rare drops that carry your bloodline further.

✦One-thumb, offline

Portrait, single-stick controls, and fully playable offline. Your save lives on your device — no account, no sign-in.

About
